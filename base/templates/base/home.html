About Me


Jason is currently a student studying computer science at Central Connecticut State University. During his time he has studied programming languages including Java, C, and Python and advanced computer science topics such as data structures and algorithms.


He enjoys studying Software Engineering and project management topics using agile, and has experience managing a team using agile methodology. Jason also is passionate about UX Design and web development, where he creates web applications for side projects.


Outside of school Jason ran Division 1 track for four years at Central Connecticut State University, he also enjoys snowboarding, fishing, hiking, and traveling in his free time.
